Edgehead

This is a short prototype of an open-world book.

The idea is to provide freedom that one would expect from an open-world RPG game — simulated environment, autonomous AI agents, resources, huge worlds to explore — but instead of rendering all that in 2D or 3D graphics, use prose.

You can learn more about the ideas behind this on egamebook.com.

I would love to hear your feedback. Please send me an email with your comments, critique or bug reports.

About this demo

This demo is purposefully very short and brutal. It is meant for a few replays — so that you can experience just how different things can get.

The situation presented in the demo is simplistic. But please imagine this situation to be a small part of a much larger quest, with actual story, exploration, and strategy. The idea that this prototype is trying to test is that storytelling is ‘fractal.’ In this demo, you zoom in and out of the different levels of the combat situation. In the finished product, you'll be zooming in and out from the over-arching story arch on one end to micro-actions on the other end.
